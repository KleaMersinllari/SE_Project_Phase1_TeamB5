# Project Details
## Project Title
Room4U
## Project Statement:
Students who move to Tirana for university studies often struggle to find affordable and reliable housing. There is no centralized platform that provides accurate information about available apartments, trustworthy landlords, or potential roommates. As a result, students face high living costs, limited options, and difficulty connecting with others to share accommodation, leading to inefficient and stressful housing arrangements.
## Proposed Solution:
Our idea is to develop a mobile application that connects students looking for accommodation in Tirana in a simple, and collaborative way. The platform will allow students to create profiles, search for available apartments, and connect with others who are looking for roommates in the same area or university. The app will have a chatbox where students can communicate with other peers making it easier to form groups and rent housing together, also it will provide a marketplace where landlords can list apartments or rooms with clear details on pricing, location, and availability.
## Project Scope:
-Aim:
• To develop a user-friendly mobile application that helps students in Tirana find affordable housing and compatible roommates through a centralized and reliable platform.
-Objectives:
1.Enable students to connect and communicate with potential roommates based on location, university, or preferences
2.Provide a centralized platform for listing available apartments, rooms, and shared housing options
3.Reduce housing costs by facilitating shared rentals among students
4.Improve transparency by displaying accurate information about pricing, location, and availability
5.Simplify and speed up the process of finding accommodation
6.Create a safe and supportive student community through in-app communication features
7.Ensure an easy-to-use interface for both students and property owners
## Application Description:
The proposed application is a mobile platform designed to help students in Tirana easily find affordable accommodation and suitable roommates. It combines social networking features with a housing marketplace, creating a centralized space where students can explore housing options and connect with others.
The main users of the application are students, who search for housing, connect with roommates, and rent apartments; landlords, who list properties and manage bookings; and the administrator (admin), who oversees the platform, verifies listings, monitors user activity, and ensures the system runs smoothly.
The application includes user profiles where students can add their preferences, budget, and location. It enables roommate matching so students can connect with others who have similar needs or are located nearby. A chat feature allows users to communicate and organize shared housing. Landlords can create and manage property listings, while students can search and filter options based on price, location, and preferences. Additionally, the administrator has tools to verify users and listings, manage reports, and maintain a safe and reliable environment for all users.
